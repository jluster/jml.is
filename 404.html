Oh, shit, 404

Yeah, it happens. The web, much as we'd like it to be, isn't as static as we thought. So things disappear. You have three ways of finding them:

  • Go to web.archive.org and enter the URL you originally used to get here. There's a real chance that it'll be on there.
  • Send me an email and I'll import the old post into Jekyll and notify you as soon as that happens.
  • Enjoy what's left and new.
